SERVICES

We Provide Better

MECHANICAL WORKS

We are covering everything from engines to plumbing. We specialize in propellers, shaft alignment, shaft seal service, stern and arenson drivers, cutlass bearing replacements, and watermaker installations. We also excel in steering overhauls, equipment maintenance (hatchways, doors, swim platforms), and various lifting devices (cranes, davits, platform lifts). With expertise in gearbox servicing, CAT, MAN, MTU, VOLVO, CUMMINS engines, and generator installations (Kohler, Fisher, Pand, Paguro), we ensure top-notch performance. Our services extend to pump overhauls (fuel, fresh water, black water) and hydraulic systems, guaranteeing smooth sailing for your yacht.

CARPENTRY WORKS

All kind of wooden interiors, panels, and cabinet interiors, as well as modifying spaces to your specifications. We excel in various interior and exterior veneer woodwork, including teak deck installation, skillfull repair, and careful replacement. Our varnishing expertise enhances both interiors and exteriors. We also offer foldable chairs crafted from beachwood for functionality and elegance. Collaborating with our fiberglass department, we provide custom solutions to make your yacht a pinnacle of marine craftsmanship and innovation.

"We Bring Elegance to Every Corner"

ELECTRICALS & ELECTRONICS WORKS

From underwater lights and touch-controlled LED sights to precise navigation and satellite antenna installations, we've got you covered. Monitor your yacht's vital signs with fluid level measuring equipment and take command with yacht controllers and expert thruster cabling. Our troubleshooting experts ensure electrical issues are swiftly resolved, and we can even enhance your onboard experience with premium sound system installations. Trust us to keep your yacht at the forefront of modern technology and performance.

AIRCONDITIONING SERVICE

From installation, replacement, and repairs of ice makers, refrigerators, and air conditioning systems, including trusted brands like Marvair, Cruisair, and Webasto, we have you covered. We also excel in modifying and upgrading existing systems for luxury yachts and cruising boats, enhancing your onboard experience. Additionally, our expertise extends to all plumbing work associated with air conditioning systems, ensuring seamless functionality and reliability. Trust us to keep your yacht's climate control system in optimal condition for a smooth and enjoyable voyage.

PAINTING WORKS

Our yacht painting services are executed by a team of highly skilled painters with expertise over many years. They are proficient in handling a wide range of paint brands including Awl grip, Jotun, Standox, and Alexsesal. Our capabilities extend to encompass various gel coat repairs, with the added advantage of an in-house paint room facility. Inside our spacious facility, we can expertly paint vessels of up to 35 feet in length. Additionally, our craftsmanship extends beyond yacht exteriors, encompassing diverse wood painting techniques such as faux finish, glazes, wood staining, matte finishes and more.

"Your yacht's aesthetic transformation is in expert hands"

ANTI-FOULING WORKS

Seeking expert advice for yacht anti-fouling? Our team collaborates closely with underwater paint manufacturers, offering tailored applications. With extensive experience, we excel in osmosis repair and moisture trap solutions. We cater to fiberglass boats, aluminum yachts, and wooden vessels, providing international-standard Jotun and copper coatings. Additionally, we employ prop speed applications for propellers and running gears, ensuring optimal performance.

"Trust us for comprehensive anti-fouling expertise to safeguard your yacht"

UPHOLSTERY WORKS

Elevate your yacht's interior and exterior experience with our premium upholstery works. Whether you desire a simple upgrade or a complete makeover, our craftsmanship will transform your yacht. Our upholstery solutions cover every aspect of yacht furnishing, utilizing top-quality materials sourced from Italian brand ITALVIPLA and German brand SCHAEFFERTEC. We specialize in enhancing yacht interiors and exteriors, from luxurious cushions and sunbeds to protective covers and stylish wall panel fabrics. Make your yacht journey a true masterpiece of comfort and style with our tailored upholstery services.

FIBERGLASS WORKS

We offer custom solutions, such as dash customization for new electric equipment, live wells, rod and rack storage compartments, tackle drawers, and bait stations tailored to your preferences. We utilize advanced materials like Polyester resins, Epoxy resins (West System), and Carbon fiber for top-quality fiberglass work. Our expertise covers repairing minor cracks and holes, reinforcing stringers and floors, and working with solid and cored fiberglass laminates. We also excel in upgrading soles and decks, hardware installation, and keel and rubber repairs, ensuring your yacht is in optimal condition and tailored to your needs.

CLEANING & POLISHING

Elevate your yacht's beauty with our valeting, cleaning, and polishing services. We handle the hard work, allowing you to enjoy your time on the water. Our eco-friendly marine valet products restore, clean, polish, and protect all surfaces, maximizing your boat's luster. Our services include regular washing, waxing, polishing, sealant application, teak treatment, underwater cleaning, antifouling, and engine bay maintenance. We also offer rust removal, fender and canopy cleaning, and leather, plastic, or vinyl restoration. Plus, professional carpet and laundry service to keep your yacht in top shape.
